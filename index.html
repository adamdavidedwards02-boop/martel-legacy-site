For Residents

A rental should feel like home, not like a constant battle with your landlord.

  • Clear, upfront communication
  • Well-maintained, clean units
  • Fair, market-informed rents — not price gouging
  • Respect for your time, privacy, and family
  • Local ownership, not a distant call centre

Martel Legacy focuses on long-term relationships with residents, which means taking care of the property and the people in it. When units are improved, the goal is to add comfort and stability, not just squeeze out the highest possible dollar.

What you can expect

  • Simple, clear lease agreements
  • Reasonable expectations on both sides
  • Prompt attention to maintenance issues
  • Units that are safe, functional, and welcoming

For Investment Partners

Steady, boring, and very intentional. That’s the point.

Real assets, real people

The focus is on residential properties where demand is durable and residents actually want to stay. Each property is chosen with the basics in mind: location, numbers that make sense, and room to improve.

Conservative, long-term strategy

Martel Legacy favours long-term buy & hold and responsible leverage. The goal isn’t flashy swings — it’s predictable cash flow, carefully managed renovations, and sustainable growth over time.

Resident-first, return-conscious

By treating residents fairly and focusing on livability, turnover stays lower and buildings stay healthier. That’s good for the people living there and, quietly, good for investor outcomes too.

What Martel Legacy looks for

  • Solid working-class neighbourhoods
  • Undervalued or under-managed buildings
  • Clear plan to increase comfort & efficiency
  • Numbers that work without extreme assumptions

How partnerships typically work

Each deal is structured on its own merits, with clear roles and aligned incentives. If you’re an investor who cares about both returns and how those returns are created, a conversation might be worth having.
